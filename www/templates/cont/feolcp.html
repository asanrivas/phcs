Criteria for EOLCP
To activate EOLCP, patient must be presented with at least TWO of the following and in consultation of a doctor
Terminal discharge from hospital Terminal condition had been informed by doctor Irreversible condition Poor conscious level Unable to take orally/Only able to take sips of fluid Deteriorating vital signs
Initial Patient Assessment
Physical Condition to be completed by a doctor or a nurse
Unable to swallow Conscious Confused Restless Nausea Vomiting Distended Bladder * Diarrhea / Constipation Fever * Respiratory tract secretion / Death Rattle Peripheral cyanosis Sign and Save
